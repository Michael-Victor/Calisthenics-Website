What is Calisthenics?

Calisthenics or Callisthenics is a form of strength training consisting of a variety of movements that exercise large muscle groups, such as running, standing, grasping, pushing, etc. These exercises are often performed rhythmically and with minimal equipment, as bodyweight exercises.

ABOUT US

Calisthenics Community is such a place where people feel comfortable with failing at accomplishing their daily fitness goal just as often as they succeed. It is only through failure that one truly grows. So, building a fitness community is basically building a place where failure is not the end of the world. And to promote and create a positive calisthenics environment, we organize events at an international level. And we have various local training groups too!
